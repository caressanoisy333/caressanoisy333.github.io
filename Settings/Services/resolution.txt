# ============================================================
#   CAMERA RESOLUTION GUIDE (the 4 spec boxes)
#   Each block separated by a BLANK LINE.
#   MP = the headline number, RES = pixel resolution, NOTE = short blurb.
# ============================================================

MP: 16MP
RES: ~4920 × 3280
NOTE: Maximum detail for large lots and premium, specialized setups.

MP: 12MP
RES: ~4000 × 3000
NOTE: Zoom in and crop details like plates or faces over larger areas.

MP: 8MP
RES: ~3840 × 2160 (4K)
NOTE: Crisp ultra-HD detail. The gold standard for most homes and businesses.

MP: 5MP
RES: ~2560 × 1920
NOTE: Great everyday clarity and storage-efficient. A solid entry-to-mid choice.
